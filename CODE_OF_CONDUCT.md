# Code of Conduct

ArDali WebMedia welcomes contributors who want to build a useful, respectful Linux media application.

## Our Standards

Please:

- Be respectful and patient.
- Focus feedback on the code, behavior, or proposal.
- Assume good intent, but ask for clarification when needed.
- Keep discussions useful for maintainers and contributors.
- Respect user privacy and security.

Please do not:

- Harass, insult, or threaten people.
- Post private information.
- Derail technical discussions with repeated off-topic comments.
- Submit malicious code, hidden telemetry, credential collection, or unsafe plugin behavior.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, or remove comments and may block contributors who repeatedly violate these standards.

If you see a problem, contact the maintainer with links, screenshots, or a short description of what happened.
